Discounts
Offer discounts at checkout to encourage customers to pay with Bitcoin

Bitcoin payments can save merchants several percentage points compared with traditional payment methods, due to lower fees and zero chargeback fraud. One way to encourage customers to pay using Bitcoin is to pass on some of those savings to customers, by offering a discount for bitcoin purchases.

Setting up discounts using Coinbase merchant tools is very simple. You can enable and disable discounts at any time, and all future orders will be automatically updated to use your latest discount settings.

Discounts will be applied to all payments except for donations (as a discounted donation would simply result in a smaller donation.)

Setting Up Discounts (requires sign in)

1. Check the box for Discount on the página de configuración comercial



2. Choose a Discount Amount


You may choose a discount percentage between 0.5% and 25.0% from the scroll-down menu. The example above shows a discount of 1.0%.

Note that the discount amount that you enable here will be used for all payment buttons linked to your merchant account (except donations.) It is currently not possible to limit the discount to specific items.


3. (Optional) Show a discount badge on merchant buttons


Checking the box for showing a discount badge generates a green badge on your merchant buttons, that displays the percentage discount. These badges are a great way to alert customers about the bitcoin discount that you're offering.


4. Save changes

Your discount set up is complete! You can make changes or disable discounts at any time, and all future orders will be automatically updated.
