Implementations Engineer
San Francisco, CA

 Responsibilities

  • Source and engage merchants to integrate with the Coinbase platform; sometimes identify and explore new market opportunities (eg industries, geographies)
  • Collaborate and provide technical support with the merchant relations team to complete partner integrations
  • Develop and drive merchant relationships to encourage their ongoing success
  • Share market feedback with product and engineering leaders

 Requirements

  • Experience engaging with counterparts at Fortune 500 companies
  • Prior experience integrating the Coinbase API
  • Comfortable working with a variety of languages and understanding foreign codebases to faciliate smooth integrations with merchant partners
  • Track record of gaining adoption of new products in emerging industries
  • Strong knowledge of, and passion for, bitcoin consumer and merchant services
  • Ability to travel 25% of the time
  • Payments processing industry experience
  • Technical education (Computer Science or Computer Engineering degree preferred)

Preferences

  • Experience integrating technical products in sales, consulting, or engineering capacity
  • Enterprise software sales or other complex, technical sales experience
  • Strong academic record or other evidence of intelligence
  • Research analysis and financial modeling experience

What to send

  • Resume or detailed LinkedIn profile, links to any social media profiles
  • Whatever you think best shows your strengths - something you've built, or a story of a new business development experience
  • A brief answer to: Who's the first merchant you would onboard to Coinbase and how would you do it?
